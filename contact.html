FAQ

Common
Questions

How does online coaching actually work?

Sessions happen via FaceTime or video call. You film your swing on the range, we hop on a call, and I break everything down in real time using performance technology. It's as effective as in-person — sometimes more so.

What skill level do you work with?

All levels — from beginners who just picked up a club to competitive players chasing scratch. The plan is custom no matter where you start.

What's included in the free first month?

Everything in your chosen plan — full onboarding, game assessment, personalized plan, and your coaching sessions for the month. No catch, no hidden fees.

Can I change plans after I start?

Absolutely. Life changes and so do schedules. You can move between monthly, bi-weekly, and weekly plans at any time with no penalty.

What equipment do I need?

Just your phone, your clubs, and access to a driving range. No fancy equipment required — though if you have a swing camera or training aids, we'll use those too.

How soon can I start?

Fast. Once you reach out Conor will set up your intro call within 24 hours and you can typically start your first session within the same week.
